# Public Expression Reference

When turning graph work into public content, do not lead with technical plumbing.

## Prefer

- content graph
- content brain
- knowledge map
- public content signals
- review
- judgment
- workflow
- content boundary
- surprising connection
- current gap
- deliverable

## Avoid

- automated data extraction
- automatic growth
- viral-copy cloning
- guaranteed traffic
- fake screenshots
- private data exposure

## Good Public Angles

- "I turned scattered notes into a content graph."
- "I stopped treating AI chats as isolated threads."
- "A graph makes repeated ideas, missing links, and next deliverables visible."
- "The point is not to save everything. The point is to see what connects."

## Boundary

Do not publish private source material unless the user explicitly asks. Public posts can discuss the method learned from private material without exposing the material itself.
